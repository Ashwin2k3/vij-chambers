Our History

The Vij Chambers was founded in April 2019 in the leadership of Deepakshi Jain Vij providing end-to-end legal solutions, covering all the cross-sections of Civil Litigation including Matrimonial cases, Land disputes, succession issues, Issues related to property, Issues to individuals, Companies and Institutions ,Intellectual property, Contact Drafting, Alternate Dispute Resolution, and Competition Law having legal problems having its office in Gurgaon and New Delhi.

The Chamber has an extensive intellectual infrastructure in all aspects related to trademark registrations, copyright registrations and IP monetisation, as well as licensing and franchising. The Chamber deals in a broad range of issues and subjects having wider applications besides, trademarks, copyrights, trade names, trade secrets, geographical indications, foreign collaborations, licensing and franchising, assignments etc. and handles each and every area thereof including their registrations, oppositions, tracking of infringements and passing off, falsification, investigation etc.
