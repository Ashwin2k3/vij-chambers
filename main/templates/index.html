About the Founder

Deepakshi Jain Vij

Mrs. Deepakshi Jain Vij, the founder, brings over a decade of experience to the legal field. She has carved out a niche in IPR laws, contract drafting, legal document vetting, and addressing matrimonial and domestic violence issues.

Her extensive experience in client relations and problem-solving, coupled with her steadfast commitment, has driven her to establish an independent practice where her knowledge and expertise shine.

As a dedicated professional, Mrs. Jain has undertaken significant raids against infringement matters both within and outside Delhi, working closely with local commissioners and state police. Her practice spans the High Court of Delhi, District Courts, IPAB, and the Trademark Registry.

Supported by a diligent team of court staff and paralegals, Mrs. Jain ensures excellence and dedication in every aspect of her practice.

Our Services

  • Trademark: Legal protection for brand names, logos, and slogans.
  • Copyright: Protection for original works of authorship, such as literature, music, and art.
  • Patent: Protection for new inventions and technological advancements.
  • Design: Protection for the aesthetic design of products.

  • Domestic Violence: Legal protection and remedies for victims of domestic abuse.
